# FOSS-Hackathon

Project Title

AI-Powered Smart Student Intelligence & Opportunity Platform

1. Project Overview
   
The AI-Powered Smart Student Intelligence & Opportunity Platform is a fullstack application designed to help students track their academic journey, analyze their skills, and receive AI-powered career recommendations and opportunities.

The platform acts as a centralized digital profile and career intelligence system where students can maintain their academic records, certifications, projects, internships, and extracurricular achievements. 

Using technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing, the system analyzes this data to generate personalized career insights, skillgap analysis, and opportunity matching.

Instead of students manually searching for jobs, internships, or hackathons, the platform automatically recommends opportunities based on their skills, interests, and career goals.

This project solves a major real-world problem where students often lack clarity about career paths and miss relevant opportunities due to scattered information and poor skill alignment.

2. Problem Statement
   
Students today face several challenges:

Lack of a central platform to maintain their academic and skill profile

Difficulty identifying the right career path

Missing internships, hackathons, and job opportunities

Limited awareness of skills required by companies

Difficulty tracking personal growth and achievements At the same time, recruiters and institutions struggle to identify students whose skills match industry requirements.

3. Proposed Solution
   
This project provides a Smart AI-driven ecosystem that:

Creates a dynamic student profile

Tracks learning and achievement history

Uses AI to analyze skills and predict career paths

Suggests companies and roles suitable for each student

Matches students with jobs, internships, hackathons, and research opportunities

The system functions as an AI career mentor + opportunity discovery engine.

# FOSS-Hackathon

Project Title
AI-Powered Smart Student Intelligence & Opportunity Platform

1. Project Overview
The AI-Powered Smart Student Intelligence & Opportunity Platform is a fullstack application designed to help students track their academic journey,
analyze their skills, and receive AI-powered career recommendations and
opportunities.
The platform acts as a centralized digital profile and career intelligence system
where students can maintain their academic records, certifications, projects,
internships, and extracurricular achievements. Using technologies such as
Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Natural Language Processing,
the system analyzes this data to generate personalized career insights, skillgap analysis, and opportunity matching.
Instead of students manually searching for jobs, internships, or hackathons, the
platform automatically recommends opportunities based on their skills,
interests, and career goals.
This project solves a major real-world problem where students often lack
clarity about career paths and miss relevant opportunities due to scattered
information and poor skill alignment.
